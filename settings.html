About

ChatUI is a Bootstrap 5 demo with one-to-one and group chats, contacts, file sending, online users, and voice/video call modals. Includes authentication pages.

Switch data-bs-theme on <html> to change light/dark, and dir to switch LTR/RTL. The navbar buttons do this for you and persist in localStorage.
